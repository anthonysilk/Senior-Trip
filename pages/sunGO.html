Goings on: Sunday

At Disneyland: Mickey Tag!

Are you the top Mouser? Find and "tag" your target before you get tagged yourself. If you tag the person who was supposed to tag you, you're the winner! Use your sticker (given out with your Disneyland ticket) as your tag. Be sure to follow the rules. Prizes will be awarded.

At Disneyland: Disney Trivia Contest

Think you know more Disney trivia than Mr. Draper? Join the Disney Trivia Challenge. Teams of 1 to 4 people can compete. Pick up a entry form from Mr. Silk once we get to Disneyland. 1 point for each correct answer. No internet allowed! Turn in your form when we get back on the bus. Prizes will be awarded!

9 pm (or on return from Disney): Pizza and Ice Cream Party

Still hungry after a day at the park? Join us in the Sand Dollar Room (where you got your luggage) for some pizza, ice cream and soda provided by Mr. and Mrs. Keller. Share your favorite stories from the day and show off your Disney souvenirs!
